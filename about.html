Grape App — A New-School Technology to Meet Your Old-School Friends

Grape App is a free application for iOS (coming soon to Google Play). Grape is an easy way to meet up with friends, hang out, and do things together. Designed to simplify your fast-paced social life, Grape replaces impersonal online communication with a fun way of staying in touch with the friends you want to spend time with.

Grape App allows you to centralize all of your social activities into one simple app. No more sorting through misplaced emails, invites, texts, voice messages, and chats just to get a couple of good friends together at the same time and the same place. Invite your friends to hang out in a Bunch or join theirs. Those who need to know will know, and those that don’t...won’t.

The Who, What, When, and Where...Right Now

How do you usually invite friends to a party? Call each one of them individually? Text? Create an event on the web and wait for a response? Not much of a party when half your fiends get lost on the way, mix up the time, or forget it’s even happening.

Grape simplifies the «who, what, where, and when» of hanging out. With a few taps it’ll be easy to pick a place, invite the friends you want, map their locations in real time and see an estimate of how long it’ll take them to get there. And if there’s confusion, the integrated chat feature keeps everyone in the loop.

Grape is not just for inviting friends—it’s also for joining them. Look at your live feed and decide where you want to go and with whom. Ask to be let into a Bunch and enjoy meeting up with your friends without the stress.

Easy, Intuitive, and Just Plain Fun

You’re busy. Your friends are busy. So get busy with Grape. No more spending twenty minutes just trying to get everyone on the same page for an hour-long lunch.

Create a Grape profile and then add your friends to make a Bunch. Mix and match your Bunch depending on who you want to hang out with. Plan movies, dinners, parties, clubbing, study groups, poetry readings, concerts, hikes, shopping sprees, basketball games — anything really. There’s no limit. Real time location features, automatic reminders and chat rooms keep you connected so you’re always on top of your busy and dynamic social life. Fun emotostickers enhance your chats to make messaging even better.

We are delighted that you’ve downloaded Grape and are ready to join your friends in a Bunch. To help you familiarize yourself with the app, we’ve prepared a get-started-guide!

If you want to read it, click here to know more about how to get started.

  1. Download the application from the App Store (link) and install it, if you haven’t already done so.
  2. Open the app on your phone. Tap «Sign up» to begin your account creation process (screenhots)! Use one of your social networking accounts (Facebook, Twitter, or Foursquare) or just fill in your personal data (email, nickname and password — show it all in the screenshot) on our form.

  3. Set up your settings. Go to your profile, click settings. Here you can add your picture and theme for your profile (settings screenshot) and set up you Profile Privacy or go to Personal Info and push notifications settings.

  4. Personal info: fill in your first and last name, gender, and city. This information will not appear in your profile, but it helps us get to know you better so we can suggest friends and places to go.

  5. Push notifications. By default all pushed notifications are turned on. You can adjust this option in settings.

  6. Here you can manage the privacy of your profile:

    Keep in mind, that you can manage the privacy of your bunches while creating them. To learn more, see our Privacy Policy (link).

  7. Invite your friends:

    Grape automatically looks for your friends in your phone book. You can also find out who is using Grape with your Facebook, Twitter, and Foursquare account. You can invite your friends to use grape via email, Facebook, Twitter, and text-messages.

  8. Once you have set up your account and invited your friends, you can create your first Bunch. Press the central button inside the app to create Bunch. Invite your friends, think of a name for the Bunch, pick a time, press Done, and your friends will receive the invitation. Here you can see how a Bunch looks once it’s been created:
